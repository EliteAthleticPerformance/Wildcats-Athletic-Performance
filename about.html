About Elite Athletic Performance

A modern performance tracking system designed for schools, coaches, and athletes to measure, analyze, compare and improve athletic development.

🏆 What This System Does

Elite Athletic Performance is a multi-school athlete tracking platform that allows coaches to collect, store, and analyze performance data across strength, speed, power, and explosiveness.

  • 📊 Combine testing data tracking
  • 📈 Athlete performance profiles with radar charts
  • 🏅 Leaderboards and rankings
  • ⚡ Real-time data powered by Google Sheets
  • 🎨 Custom branding for each school

🎯 Who It's For

  • High School Strength & Conditioning Programs
  • Football / Athletic Departments
  • Performance Coaches & Trainers
  • Teams tracking combine metrics year-round
