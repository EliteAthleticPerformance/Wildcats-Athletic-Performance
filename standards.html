Standards Alignment – Elite Athletic Performance Program

This program aligns with national physical education standards by combining structured training, performance tracking, and measurable athlete development.

🏫 National PE Standards Alignment

Standard 1: Motor Skills & Movement

Students perform and refine fundamental and advanced movement patterns including strength lifts, agility drills, and coordination-based exercises.

Standard 2: Knowledge of Concepts

  • Sets, reps, and intensity
  • Percentage-based lifting
  • Movement efficiency and technique

Standard 3: Physical Activity & Fitness

  • Timed training rotations
  • Strength and conditioning sessions
  • Movement-based circuits

Standard 4: Personal & Social Behavior

  • Responsibility in group training environments
  • Safe equipment usage
  • Collaboration and teamwork during rotations

Standard 5: Value of Physical Activity

  • Goal setting
  • Self-motivation
  • Long-term fitness habits

📊 Assessment Methods

  • Performance metrics (strength, speed, agility)
  • Progress tracking over time
  • Participation and effort
  • Skill development

✅ Conclusion

The Elite Athletic Performance program meets and exceeds national PE standards by combining structured physical activity with measurable outcomes and individualized instruction.
